ABOUT US

PLATFORM

We provide the members of the pride community, a safe environment to expand their small businesses. Owners can list and sell their products without any fear as everyone is welcome aboard!

We also provide a separate page for all the notifications regarding the upcoming events related to the pride community. And the best part? EVERYONE IS WELCOME!

CONNECT

We aim to build a community with like-minded people, be it allies or the people of the community, who can connect with each other. We provide a separate page for blogs where people can write about any experience that they faced, be it their, coming-out stories or any wholesome incident that helped them in difficult times.

SUPPORT US!

Want to make a change but don't know how? No worries, we got you covered! Apart from supporting the small business's, you also have option to give to the community by donating any amount you prefer to any NGO of your choice from the given links!
